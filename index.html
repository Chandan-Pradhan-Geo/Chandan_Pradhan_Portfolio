I’m Chandan Pradhan, a postgraduate student in Applied Geology at IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, with a strong interest in geological modeling, geostatistics, and subsurface exploration. My current research focuses on the Iron Ore Mineralization at Razorback Ridge, Southern Australia, where I’m developing integrated 3D geological and geostatistical models to better understand ore body characteristics. I enjoy combining Earth sciences with coding to build smarter, data-driven geological insights using tools like Leapfrog, QGIS, and Python. I’m always exploring ways to bring tech and geology together for better decision-making in mining and exploration.

My Best Services

Geological Modeling

I build detailed geological and geostatistical models of ore deposits using software like Leapfrog and Edge, enabling accurate interpretation and planning.

Drill Hole Logging

I specialize in lithological and geotechnical core logging, delivering high-quality data for exploration and resource estimation.

Resource Estimation

I apply geostatistical methods to estimate mineral resources and model grade distributions across complex geological terrains.

QA/QC Practices

Ensuring data accuracy and quality through standard QA/QC protocols, helping maintain exploration reliability and confidence.

GIS & Remote Sensing

Spatial analysis using QGIS and satellite data to assist in terrain mapping, anomaly detection, and geological interpretations.

Field Mapping

Conducting structural and lithological mapping in the field to generate accurate geological maps and cross-sections for project reports.
